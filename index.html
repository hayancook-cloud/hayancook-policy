HayanCook TikTok Upload Integration

This web page demonstrates the user-approved workflow for connecting the HayanCook owner’s TikTok account and uploading short-form cooking videos through TikTok’s official Login Kit and Content Posting API.

1. Connect TikTok Account

The account owner manually starts OAuth and grants TikTok permissions. The app does not connect or post without user approval.

Status: TikTok account is not connected yet. Scopes requested for review: user.info.basic, video.upload, video.publish
For app-review recording: click “Connect TikTok Account” after entering the TikTok Client Key. TikTok will open the official OAuth authorization screen.

2. Select Video and Caption

The user selects a specific video and reviews the caption before any upload request is submitted.

Upload status: waiting for user-selected video.

End-to-end review flow shown in the demo video

  1. Open this web page from the Web/Desktop URL submitted to TikTok.
  2. Click “Connect TikTok Account” and complete TikTok OAuth authorization.
  3. Return to the upload interface and confirm the connected account state.
  4. Select a user-approved MP4/MOV cooking video.
  5. Enter or confirm the caption before publishing.
  6. Click “Upload to TikTok” to submit the user-approved upload request.
  7. Show the final upload status or TikTok processing result to the user.

This page is for HayanCook owner-operated publishing. It does not perform automatic posting, bulk posting, scraping, commenting, liking, following, or direct messaging.
