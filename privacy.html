HayanCook Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

HayanCook respects your privacy.

HayanCook does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information through this TikTok developer application. The application may be used to connect HayanCook’s official TikTok account for content posting and creator account management.

When you interact with HayanCook content on third-party platforms such as TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, or Notion, those platforms may collect and process your data according to their own privacy policies.

If TikTok API access is used, only the minimum required information for authentication, account identification, and video publishing may be processed. Access tokens or account credentials are stored securely and are not shared publicly.

HayanCook does not sell personal information to third parties.

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us through the official HayanCook social media account.
